Welcome to the New Hampshire License Plate Museum.

This site contains pictures of may of the Museum's License Plate panels, showing type runs of New Hampshire and a few Maine license plates. Many runs still have plates missing and the Museum is alwyas interested in purchasing plates to complete the panels. We hope that you will enjoy this little bit of New Hampshire's motoring history.
